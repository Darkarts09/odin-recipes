Chai Tea

Description

Chai tea, steeped in rich spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and ginger, offers a warming and aromatic experience, originating from India.

Its robust blend of black tea and fragrant spices creates a comforting brew that's beloved globally for its rich flavors and soothing qualities.

Ingredients

  • Black Tea
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Ginger
  • Cloves

Steps

  1. Boil Spices and Tea: In a pot, combine water, milk, black tea, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and fresh ginger slices. Bring it to a boil.
  2. Simmer: Once boiling, reduce heat to low and let the mixture simmer for about 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse. If you prefer a stronger flavor, you can simmer it a bit longer.
  3. Strain and Sweeten (Optional): After simmering, strain the tea into a cup or teapot to remove the spices and tea leaves. Add sweetener if desired, such as sugar or honey, and enjoy your homemade chai tea! Adjust the ingredients and sweetness level to suit your taste preferences.
